About us

A full-service landscaping company serving Augusta and the CSRA

Signature Grounds, LLC was built on a simple belief: your outdoor space deserves the same care and attention you give to everything else in your home. Whether it's weekly lawn maintenance or a full property transformation, we bring the same standard of quality to every job we take on.

We have the tools, the resources, and the expertise to handle anything your property needs — from irrigation and drainage to retaining walls, outdoor lighting, and landscape design. No project is too routine, and none is too ambitious.

We're proud to serve homeowners and businesses throughout Augusta, Evans, Martinez, and Appling, Georgia. When you hire Signature Grounds, you're getting a crew that respects your property and shows up ready to work.
